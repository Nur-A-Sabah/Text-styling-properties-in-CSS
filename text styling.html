Food, substance consisting essentially of protein, carbohydrate, fat, and other nutrients used in the body of on organism to sustain growth and vital processes and to furnish energy.
the adsorption and utilization of food by the body is fundamental to nutrition and is focilitated by digestion.
Plants, which convert solar energy to food by the photosynthesis, are the primary food source.

Animals that feed on plants often serve as sources of food for other animals.
